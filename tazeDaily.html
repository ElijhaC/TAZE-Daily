About TAZE Daily:

Hear ye, hear ye to TAZE Daily! Latest SHOCKING updates on current events, politics, entertainment, and more!

Our group created a news site called “TAZE Daily” that is similar to NY Times: TAZE Daily . Purpose is to demonstrate construction of a full-stack web-application.

    Our site has the following genres:
  • Politics
  • Business
  • Technology
  • Health
  • Sports
  • Entertainment
  • Travel
  • Food
  • Style

Features of TAZE Daily:

    As a client (not logged in)
  • See news headlines
  • See # of likes about news stories
      • As a user (logged in)
    • Search for news of interest
    • Create bookmarks for myself
    • Add a like to a news story
    • Create comments on news stories that other logged in users can see

    Developmental Notes:

    To initialize the application, we will be using Spring Initializer/JHipster. As for our tech stack, we will be using Spring, React/Bootstrap, Java, MySQL, and Render.
